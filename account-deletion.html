Account & Data Deletion

App: EuroM Predictor
Developer: EuroM Predictor Team

Option 1 — Delete your account directly in the app (recommended)

  1. Open EuroM Predictor on your device.
  2. Sign in with your account.
  3. Go to Settings (gear icon).
  4. Scroll down to the Account section.
  5. Tap "Delete my account".
  6. Confirm the action in the dialog.

Your account and all associated personal data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, send us an email from the address associated with your account:

support@eurompredictor.com

Please include:

  • The email address used for your account
  • The subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm by email.

What data is deleted

  • Email address and authentication credentials
  • User profile (display name, preferences, language)
  • Favorite combinations and saved selections
  • App usage history and analytics linked to your account
  • Subscription and premium status

What data may be retained

For legal, accounting, and fraud-prevention reasons, the following data may be retained for a limited period (up to 10 years as required by applicable law):

  • Anonymized purchase records (if any subscription was made)
  • Server logs (technical, anonymized, retained up to 90 days)

All retained data is anonymized and cannot be linked back to your identity.

⚠️ This action is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered. You will need to create a new account if you wish to use the app again.
