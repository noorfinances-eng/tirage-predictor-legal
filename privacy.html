Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Data collection

This app does not collect any personally identifiable data. All statistics and data are generated locally on your device.

2. Local storage

The app uses your device's local storage to save your preferences, draw history, favorite combinations, and other usage data. This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to third-party servers.

3. Analytics and statistics

Statistical analyses are calculated locally from public EuroMillions and Swiss Loto draw data. No information about your usage is shared with third parties.

4. Cookies

This app does not use tracking cookies. Preferences are stored locally to enhance your user experience.

5. Security

We take the security of your data seriously. All data is stored securely on your device. Authentication, when used, is handled through Firebase Authentication with industry-standard encryption.

6. Third-party services

The app may contain links to third-party services (such as official lottery websites). These services have their own privacy policies that we encourage you to review. The app uses Firebase (Google) for optional authentication and cloud-synced draw data.

7. Children's privacy

This app is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18.

8. Changes

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new update date.

9. Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, you can contact us through the app settings or via the support email listed on the app store page.
